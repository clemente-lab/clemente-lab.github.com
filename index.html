--- redirect_to: "http://clementelab.org" layout: page title: "Welcome" headline: Get to know us... description: "Developing computational methods to analyze our microbial world." tags: [home, bioinformatics, computational-biology] --- The Clemente Lab is part of the Department of Genetics and Genomic Sciences and the Immunology Institute at Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in New York City. We are broadly interested in bioinformatics and computational biology, particularly with a focus on characterizing the microbiome in human subjects and in the environment.
